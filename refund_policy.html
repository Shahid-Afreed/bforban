Refund Policy

At BFORBAN, we are committed to providing our esteemed customers with premium-quality products and an exceptional shopping experience. If for any reason you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, we are here to assist you. Please review the following policy for details on returns, refunds, and exchanges.

Returns & Exchanges

Eligibility for Returns: To qualify for a return, the product must be in its original, unused condition, and in the original packaging. It must not have been altered, damaged, or washed.

Return Window: You have a 30-day period from the date of receipt to initiate a return request. After this time frame, we regret that we will be unable to process returns.

Exchanges: In the rare event that you receive a damaged or defective product, we are pleased to offer an exchange. Please notify us within 1 week of receipt.

Refund Process

Requesting a Refund: Should you wish to request a refund, kindly reach out to our dedicated customer service team. Please provide your order number, reason for the return, and relevant images or documentation to support your claim.

Refund Approval: Upon receipt of your returned item, we will thoroughly inspect it to ensure it meets the eligibility criteria. If your return is approved, a refund will be processed to your original method of payment. Please note that processing times may vary depending on your payment provider.

Refund Timeline: After approval, the refund will be credited to your account within 7–10 business days. Should there be any delays, we kindly request your patience as processing times may vary.

Partial Refunds: In certain circumstances, partial refunds may be issued for items that are not in their original condition (e.g., if damaged or used). Our team will notify you should this be the case.

Shipping Costs

Return Shipping: The customer is responsible for any return shipping costs unless the item is defective or incorrect. We recommend using a traceable and insured shipping service for returns.

Free Shipping Orders: If your order qualified for free shipping, the original shipping cost will be deducted from your refund in the event of a return for any reason other than a defective or incorrect product.

Late or Missing Refunds

Check with your bank or credit card company: It may take some time before your refund is officially posted.

Contact your payment provider: There may be processing delays on their end.
